Carriles Bici Bus

Carriles Bici-Bus – From spanish. Trans: Bike-Bus Lanes – is a project made for MetroCali (Cali, Colombia transportation network company ).

In this project, MetroCali was introducing a new service. The “Carriles Bici-Bus”, special lanes for both bikes and buses. MetroCali needed it to incorporate this new system to the transportation network. In order to do that, they required a new transportation map for the system and a manual that explained the users how to use the special lanes.

Location: Cali-Colombia

2015
