Caméya

Caméya – From Colombian coloquialism Camellar and word 'ya'. Trans: Work now – is an job “matching” app for ex-guerrilla members and entrepreneurs in Colombia.
Colombia is going through a transition. Guerilla people are returning to civil society. In order to do so, they are in need of jobs. Cameyá tackles this issue. Many ex-guerrilla members lack of degrees but have knowledge to do many things useful for society. Contrary to other job apps, Cameyá offers the opportunity to evaluate employee candidates based on their soft skills (how are they as person and worker) and hard skills (what abilities do they have) allowing connection employer and job hunter without barriers.

Location: Dessau - Germany
Team: Mazin Ali, Adrian Ramos.
2016

#UX/UI #Apps #Social #JobPlatform

BRIEF

Develop a digital service that constructs a bridge between ex-Farc members and Colombian civilians. Allowing the formers to reintegrate society while creating platform for people to work and live together.

Design Process

We started our design process attempting to understand the life and needs of our users. 'Tania', 'Jeison' and 'Ines' became our personas and inspiration for the rest of the process.
After some brainstorming sessions, we decided on an idea to be later developed into a solid mental model. The later dictated all the further design and prototyping decisions.
