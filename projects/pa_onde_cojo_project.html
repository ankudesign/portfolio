Pa' onde cojo?

Pa' onde cojo? – From spanish. Trans: Where should I go? – is a touristic guide map based on Bogotá’s most important cultural aspects. It contains information on bar, cafes and restaurants recommended by locals. ¿Pa ‘onde cojo? invites visitors to enjoy the most unique aspects of Bogota, explore the landscapes that local appreciate every day. Thus, its content and its design are targeted to appreciate the cultural, artisanal, the metropolis, the colonial Bogota.

Location: Bogotá-Colombia

2012
